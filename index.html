GR86 Highlights

The Engine.

The Toyota GR86 is powered by a 2.4L flat-four boxer engine by Subaru, that delivers 228 horsepower and a thrilling, high-revving driving experience.

Transmission.

The Toyota GR86 offers a choice between a close-ratio 6-speed manual for pure driving engagement and a 6-speed automatic with paddle shifters for smooth, responsive shifts.

Special Edition

Dubbed the Special Edition, just 860 are earmarked for production—wears a coat of orange paint that’s complemented by black decor and a set of matte black 18-inch wheels.

The Trueno Edition

The GR86 Trueno Edition is a retro-inspired tribute to the iconic AE86, featuring a two-tone exterior, red-accented Ultrasuede interior, and upgraded Brembo brakes with SACHS dampers for sharper handling.

The "Yuzu" Edition

A vibrant burst of citrus energy, the Yuzu Edition turns heads with its exclusive Solar Shift paint and sharp black accents—limited to just 860 units.

The Hakone Edition

Inspired by the curves of Japan’s famed Hakone Turnpike, this edition wraps the GR86 in Ridge Green with bronze wheels and a cabin built for spirited drives.

Personalisation

The GR86 is offered with a variety of OEM and after-market accessories, kits and has abundant tuning potential. Feel free to customize your ride!
